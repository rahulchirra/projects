PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Our scientifically designed 24-week program systematically builds your boxing skills, conditioning, and fight IQ through three progressive phases.

Beginner Phase

Weeks 1-8: Build fundamentals, stamina, and discipline with 4-5 training days per week.

  • Focus on proper form and technique
  • Basic footwork and punch combinations
  • Conditioning foundation

Intermediate Phase

Weeks 9-16: Develop power, sparring skills, and complex combinations with 5-6 training days.

  • Introduction to sparring
  • Advanced combinations and counters
  • Increased power and speed training

Advanced Phase

Weeks 17-24: Tournament preparation and elite conditioning with 6 training days per week.

  • Fight simulation training
  • Competition strategy development
  • Peak physical conditioning

COMPLETE TRAINING PROGRAM

Detailed weekly breakdown of all exercises, sets, reps, and rest periods for each phase.

1

Beginner Phase (Weeks 1-8)

Focus: Build fundamentals, stamina, and discipline. Training Days: 4-5 days/week.

Monday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Warm-Up 10-min jump rope - Light pace, focus on rhythm
Footwork Drills 3 sets x 2 mins 60 sec Practice advancing, retreating, pivoting
Jab-Cross (1-2) 3 sets x 30 reps (on mirror) 45 sec Focus on form, shoulder rotation
Heavy Bag 3 rounds x 2 mins 60 sec Light power, 1-2 combos only
Push-Ups 3 sets x 15 reps 60 sec Full range of motion

Wednesday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Warm-Up 10-min dynamic stretching - Arm circles, leg swings, torso twists
Hook/Uppercut Practice 3 sets x 20 reps (each arm) 45 sec Use focus mitts or heavy bag; rotate hips
Defense Drills 3 sets x 2 mins 60 sec Slips, blocks, and bobs (use a partner or rope)
Burpees 3 sets x 10 reps 60 sec Explosive jumps

Friday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Warm-Up 10-min shadowboxing - Practice stance and basic punches
Combination Drills 1-2-3 (jab-cross-hook) x 30 60 sec 3 sets on heavy bag
Bodyweight Circuit 3 rounds: 60 sec 15 push-ups, 20 squats, 1-min plank

Saturday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Endurance 30-min brisk walk/jog - Low intensity; focus on breathing
2

Intermediate Phase (Weeks 9-16)

Focus: Power, sparring, and complex combinations. Training Days: 5-6 days/week.

Monday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Warm-Up 15-min jump rope - Add footwork variations (crisscross, double-unders)
Partner Defense Drills 3 rounds x 3 mins 60 sec Block, parry, and counter 1-2 combos
Light Sparring 4 rounds x 2 mins 90 sec 30% power; focus on technique
Plyometric Push-Ups 3 sets x 12 reps 60 sec Explode upward on each rep

Wednesday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Warm-Up 10-min shadowboxing w/ 1 lb - Mimic punches with light dumbbells
Mitt Work 3 rounds x 3 mins 60 sec Complex combos (e.g., 1-2-slip-3-roll)
Heavy Bag (Power Shots) 5 rounds x 2 mins 60 sec 20-sec max effort bursts per round
Kettlebell Swings 4 sets x 20 reps 45 sec Use 15-25 lbs; hinge at hips

Friday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Speed Bag 5 rounds x 2 mins 30 sec Maintain rhythm; alternate hands
Sprints 10 x 100m 60 sec All-out effort
Core Circuit 3 rounds: 60 sec 20 Russian twists, 15 leg raises, 1-min side plank (each side)
3

Advanced Phase (Weeks 17-24)

Focus: Tournament prep, fight simulation, and elite conditioning. Training Days: 6 days/week.

Monday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Warm-Up 15-min jump rope + stretches - High intensity
Sparring 6 rounds x 3 mins 60 sec 70-80% power; simulate tournament rules
Counter-Punch Mitts 3 rounds x 3 mins 60 sec Slip → cross → hook combos
Pull-Ups 4 sets x 10 reps 60 sec Full extension at bottom

Wednesday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Double-End Bag 5 rounds x 3 mins 30 sec Focus on timing and precision
Interval Training 30 sec sprint / 30 sec jog - Repeat 15x
Medicine Ball Slams 4 sets x 25 reps 45 sec Use 10-20 lb ball; full overhead throw

Friday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Endurance Circuit 3 rounds: 120 sec 3-min heavy bag, 1-min burpees, 1-min battle ropes (no rest between)

Saturday Workout

Exercise Sets/Reps/Duration Rest Between Sets Notes
Mental Training 10-min visualization - Imagine executing perfect combos and winning rounds
Strategy Session Study opponent footage - Identify weaknesses and plan counters
